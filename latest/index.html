    LeafAreaIndex.jl

    The LeafAreaIndex.jl package offers several methods to work with hemispherical pictures for the determination of Leaf Area Index (LAI).

    The ICOS - ETC team at the University of Antwerp provides a free online service with automatic LAI estimation: https://icos.ua.ac.be/

    For more information on the organization see the website of ICOS Belgium.
